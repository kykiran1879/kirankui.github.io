Introduction

Kiran Kui, MPH (she/her) is a Research Associate II at Tufts CTSI and ADAPT Coalition with an expertise in Epidemiology and Applied Biostatistics.

Her research centers on the intersection of environmental justice, food justice, and chronic health outcomes, with the goal of informing culturally sensitive policies that alleviate health disparities in underserved populations.

Research Skills

Advanced statistical/ data analysis skills:

  • Predictive Disease Modelling using Linear, Logistic, Cluster and Cox Regression Models
  • Programming skills in R, SAS, and GitHub
  • Spatial Analysis skills in QGIS, Geoda

Nutritional Epidemiology skills:

  • Advanced Data Analysis of NHANES Dietary Datasets (2011-2020)
  • 24-hour Dietary Recalls
  • Food Frequency Questionnaires
  • Food Coding using Dietplan7
